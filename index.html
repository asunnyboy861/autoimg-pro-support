Subscription FAQ

Purchasing Subscriptions

Q: How do I purchase a subscription?
A: Click the "Pro" button in the app and select the subscription plan that suits you best. Payment is securely processed through Apple App Store.

Q: What subscription plans are available?
A: We offer 4 subscription plans:

  • Weekly: $1.49/week
  • Monthly: $4.99/month
  • Yearly: $24.99/year (Recommended, Save 58%)
  • Lifetime: $39.99 (One-time payment)

Q: Does the yearly subscription have a free trial?
A: Yes, the yearly subscription offers a 7-day free trial. You can cancel anytime during the trial period without being charged.

Q: What does the Lifetime plan include?
A: The Lifetime plan includes all Pro features with a one-time payment. No renewal required, permanent access to all features.

Managing Subscriptions

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: You can cancel your subscription at any time:

  • iOS/iPadOS: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Select ID Photo Pro → Cancel Subscription
  • macOS: App Store → Account icon → Manage Subscriptions → Select ID Photo Pro → Cancel Subscription

Q: Will my subscription automatically renew?
A: Yes, subscriptions will automatically renew. To cancel, you must turn off auto-renewal at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.

Q: Can I switch to a different subscription plan?
A: Yes, you can switch to a different subscription plan at any time. The new plan will take effect after the current billing period ends.

Q: How do I restore my purchase?
A: Click the "Restore Purchase" button on the Paywall page. The app will automatically verify your purchase history.

Q: How are refunds handled?
A: Refund policy follows Apple App Store's refund policy. Users may request a refund through the App Store within a certain period after purchase. Refund processing is handled by Apple.

Q: What happens when my subscription expires?
A: When your subscription expires:

  • The app will remind you 7 days before expiration
  • After expiration, you'll automatically revert to the free version
  • Already generated photos will still be accessible
  • You'll need to resubscribe to continue using Pro features

Q: Can I use my subscription on multiple devices?
A: Yes, all devices signed in with the same Apple ID can use the subscription.

Usage FAQ

Basic Usage

Q: How do I get started with AutoImg Pro?
A: After downloading and installing the app, follow the on-screen instructions to get started. All features include in-app usage guides.

Q: Which devices are supported?
A: AutoImg Pro supports iPhone, iPad, and Mac running iOS 14.0 or later / macOS 11.0 or later.

Q: How do I get the latest version?
A: Please check for updates in the App Store to ensure you're using the latest version for the best experience and newest features.

Q: What image formats are supported?
A: We support JPEG, PNG, HEIC, and most common image formats.

Q: Where are my photos stored?
A: All photo processing is completed locally on your device. We do not upload or store your photos on our servers.

Q: How do I export photos?
A: After processing is complete, click the "Export" button and select your save location and format (PNG/JPEG).

Q: What photo types are supported?
A: We support passport, visa, ID card, driver's license, and various other official photo types, covering multiple countries/regions.

Troubleshooting

Q: The app crashes. What should I do?
A:

  1. Restart the app
  2. Update to the latest version
  3. Restart your device
  4. If the issue persists, please contact us with the crash report

Q: Processing is slow. How can I speed it up?
A:

  1. Close other running apps
  2. Check your device's available storage space
  3. Lower the beauty enhancement level
  4. Use smaller image sizes

Q: I can't import photos. What should I do?
A:

  1. Check if the photo format is supported
  2. Check if the photo is corrupted
  3. Check if the app has permission to access photos
  4. Restart the app

Q: Export fails. What should I do?
A:

  1. Check your device's available storage space
  2. Check if you have write permission
  3. Try exporting to a different location
  4. Restart the app

Q: AI processing fails. What should I do?
A:

  1. Try re-importing the photo
  2. Check your network connection
  3. Ensure the photo has a clear face
  4. If the issue persists, please contact us
